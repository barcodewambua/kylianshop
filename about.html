Our Story

Redefining modern elegance through mindful creation and timeless design.

The Vision

A return to essentialism in fashion.

Founded in 2026, Kylian was born out of a desire for a wardrobe that speaks volumes without shouting. We believe that true style lies in simplicity, quality fabrics, and impeccable tailoring.

Our collections are deliberately concise, focusing on foundational pieces that form the bedrock of a versatile wardrobe. We reject the cycle of fast fashion, instead offering garments intended to be worn, loved, and kept for years.

Craftsmanship

Mindful creation from thread to finish.

We source our materials from sustainable mills globally, prioritizing organic cottons, recycled polyesters, and ethically sourced wools.

Partnering with family-owned factories in Portugal and Italy, we ensure that every garment is crafted by artisans who are treated fairly and share our commitment to uncompromising quality.
